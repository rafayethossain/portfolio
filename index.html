Workflow
How I Work

  • 01

    Planning and Requirement Analysis

    As I kick off the project, I carefully figure out what it's all about, how things work operationally and technically. I sketch out an initial plan. I also set up get-togethers with everyone involved, chatting and getting ideas. These steps make sure we're on the same page and everyone's in the loop for the project journey.

  • 02

    Defining Requirements and Designing the Product

    I craft user stories or use cases, diving into key business details and requirements. I break down high-level requirements into practical user stories. I participate in risk analysis, shaping the architecture, and defining criteria for acceptance. It's about translating big ideas into actionable steps and ensuring our work aligns with the broader project goals.

  • 03

    Developing the Product

    I focus on fine-tuning requirements, ensuring they're clear and precise. I play a crucial part in supporting the development process by providing clarity on requirements. Additionally, I gather any remaining user needs, making sure we capture all the essential elements to drive successful project outcomes.

  • 04

    Testing the Product

    I lend a hand to the QA team, reviewing test plans and results to ensure our work meets quality standards. I actively join in planning, conducting, and evaluating User Acceptance Testing. Additionally, I take part of planning user training sessions, ensuring everyone is well-prepared for seamless adoption of our solutions.

  • 05

    Deployment and Maintenance

    I take charge of coordinating the deployment process, ensuring a smooth integration into the customer's business environment. I support management throughout deployment, actively participating in pre-implementation readiness and post-implementation reviews. Additionally, I handle Change requests, conducting impact analysis to manage any necessary adjustments and keep the project on track.
